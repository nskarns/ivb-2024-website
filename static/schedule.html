Welcome to the Irish Volunteer Brigade
The Irish Volunteer Brigade is a regiment in the Civil War FPS War of Rights. Formed in 2019, the regiment consists of a healthy mix of old veterans new recruits. Although we’re the Irish Volunteers, you don’t have to be the Irish to join!
